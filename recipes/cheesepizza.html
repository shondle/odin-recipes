Cheese Pizza

Description

A cheese pizza is a classic pizza topped with mozzarella cheese and often served with marinara sauce. It is a simple yet delicious meal that can be customized with a variety of toppings, such as vegetables and meats, to add flavor and texture. Making a cheese pizza at home is a fun and easy activity that can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (vegetables, meats, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the marinara sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Sprinkle the cheese over the sauce and add any desired toppings.
  4. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
