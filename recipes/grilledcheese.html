Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet delicious sandwich made by grilling bread and cheese together until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted. It can be served on its own or with a variety of toppings, such as tomato or bacon, to add flavor and texture. Grilled cheese sandwiches are a quick and easy meal option that can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Toppings of your choice (tomato, bacon, etc.)

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread. Place one slice of bread, butter-side-down, in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the cheese slices and any desired toppings to the bread in the pan. Top with the other slice of bread, butter-side-up.
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom slice of bread is toasted and the cheese is melted. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the second slice of bread is toasted.
  4. Remove the sandwich from the pan and cut in half, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
